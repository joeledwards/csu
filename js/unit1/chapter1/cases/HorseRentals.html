Horse Rentals

Horses are our passion here at Horse Rentals. We offer personal and group lessons, on and off site horse rentals, and guided tours. We offer instruction and supplies for both Western and English riding styles.

Group Lessons

Those who are new to horseback riding will enjoy the gentle introduction provided by the our group lessons. Our least "spirited" horses are supplied for these sessions to ensure a pleasant introduction to all new riders. Group lessons are $45 for a 2 hour session (maximum of 10 in each group).

Personal Lessons

Riders who wish to have closer interaction with the instructor and more in-depth training will benefit from our personal lessons. These lessons are ideal for those who are not comfortable starting out on thier own, or wish to advance beyond what is provided in the Group Lessons. Personal lessons are $120 for a 2 hour session.

On-Site Rentals

Familiar and proximal terrain is easier on both horse and rider for customers are new to riding. On-site rentals are $35 for the first hour, and $20 for each additional hour.

Off-Site Rentals

Freedom to explore new places and visit favorite locations on horseback is a logical expectation of our experienced customers. Off-site rentals are $60 for the first hour, and $40 for each additional hour.

Guided Tours

Our trail guies are expert riders who will guide tours to many locations within the county. Guided tours are $85 for the first horse/hour, each additional horse is $55. Additional horse hours are $65 for the first horse, and $35 for each additional horse.
